IMPORTANT STUFF

Estimated Delivery Time is 1-2 weeks for Simple or Advanced bots, and likely more for Premium Custom bots depending on the project. Most projects will be completed far before 2 weeks, but please allow up to 2 weeks since I do have a life outside of coding.

For your bots to work you’ll need to create an account for them yourself. On Twitch, this includes creating an account for your bot, setting it up with two factor authentication, and getting its Oauth code. For Discord, this includes creating a developer account and bot user, or letting me create it and giving me admin rights on your server briefly to add it. I will explain all of this once you contact me.

You will need to host your bot somewhere. Bots don’t just work magically, sadly, and all those online bots like Nightbot are just hosted on a cloud server somewhere. For a custom bot, no matter if its for Twitch, Discord, or something else, you need to run it on a computer for it to work. For anything that interacts with your computer, you must host it ON your computer. If your bot doesn’t interact directly with your computer, you may host it yourself or anywhere else. I do offer hosting as well, info is below.

All bots include lifetime bug fixes. Any issues you might have will be resolved as quickly as possible. Remember that this includes fixing the existing features, not adding new features. You can always commission me again for new features!

HOSTING

I offer a professional hosting solution for any bots (Not just the ones I create). I host on an extremely powerful server on a rock-solid stable network I built myself, with battery backups and significant redundancy. Basically, your bot is very unlikely to encounter any sort of network issues hosted on my servers. I will handle all the setup and configuration, you just simply provide the bot files and I’ll get it connected right up for you.

Please do note that ANY bot that interacts with your computer or game cannot be hosted on any computer other than your own. This is because the interaction functionality in my bots run programs on your computer that essentially control your mouse and keyboard for a few milliseconds, and those programs can’t be run if they’re not on your computer.

Hosting starts at $2/month, and includes free setup and support through the life of your hosting subscription with as quick of a response time as I can possibly provide. Extremely intensive bots may incur extra costs.

Please contact me if you are interested in hosting!
